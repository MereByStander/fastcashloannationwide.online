LOAN PROCESS

Know if you are qualified

  • Answer some chat interview questions to help us pre-screen if you are qualified.

Submit Requirements

  • Submit pictures or scanned copies of your requirements via our email fcloannationwide@gmail.com or pm us directly on facebook.

Receive Acknowledgement and update from us

  • We will chat or call you to acknowledge or confirm the status of your application.

Phone and Video Call Interview

  • Expect phone calls and video call interviews with you and with your co-borrower.

Loan Approval

  • You will be phoned and advised regarding the amount, terms and conditions and computation of the approval loan.

E-Signing

  • Upon your acceptance of the terms and conditions, you will be emailed with the documents that you needed to sign online.

Loan Proceeds

  • After sending us back the E-Signed forms, the loan proceeds will be transferred to your bank account.
